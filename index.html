CRVENA ZVEZDA ESPORTS

WHERE HISTORY IS MADE

Crvena zvezda Esports is a Serbian team formed as part of the professional sports club Crvena zvezda, also known as Red Star Belgrade. The club is based in Belgrade, was founded in 1945, and is nicknamed "Zvezda" which means "The Star". They also have teams for football, rugby, basketball, volleyball, handball, and many other sports.
